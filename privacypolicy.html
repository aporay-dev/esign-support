Privacy Policy

eSign: PDF Fill & Signature

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Myriad Tech Labs Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the eSign: PDF Fill & Signature mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Summary: The App is designed to help users import, view, fill, sign, stamp, and share documents. Documents and signatures are processed on your device unless you choose to share or export them using iOS or third-party services.

Information We Collect

We may collect limited information needed to operate, maintain, and improve the App. This may include:

  • Usage and diagnostics data: technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Purchase and subscription status: information provided by Apple and/or our subscription management provider to confirm access to paid features.
  • Support information: information you choose to provide when contacting us for support, such as your email address, message content, screenshots, or diagnostic details.

Documents, Signatures, Initials, and Stamps

The App may allow you to import documents, create signatures, initials, stamps, and export or share completed files. These files are generally stored and processed locally on your device. We do not intentionally collect the contents of your documents, signatures, initials, or stamps unless you voluntarily send them to us when requesting support.

Permissions

The App may request access to device features such as camera, photos, files, or document picker access so you can scan, import, edit, sign, stamp, or export documents. You can manage permissions in your device settings.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and maintain App functionality, process purchases and premium access, respond to support requests, improve stability and security, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for app distribution, payments, subscriptions, analytics, crash reporting, or customer support. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the App, provide support, comply with law, protect our rights, or complete business operations such as an app transfer, merger, acquisition, or similar transaction.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Documents saved inside the App remain under your control on your device unless you export, share, or delete them.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices
